Upcoming Events

Erdh Lezeti is an already traditional Kosovar festival held every year, to celebrate the arrival of summer and the departure of winter, where hundreds of citizens gather for a pleasant musical experience, with artists and musicians from all over the world.

Erdh Lezeti

Fer Film is an international film festival, based in Kosove.The festival aims, in addition to promoting artists and film, as well as tourism in our city and region. Also, the purpose of the festival is to have a regional character in the future.

Fer Film

ReMusica Festival is one of the main pillars of cultural life in Kosovo, with a specific focus on classical / modern music.The Festival has had a very important role in promotion of Kosovar artists, and culture through very close and cooperation with prominent international artists.

Re Musica

DokuFest, International Short Film and Documentary Festival, is the largest film festival in Kosove. Each year the festival brings the cinemas and improvised screening venues around the historic city of Prizren a selection of more than 200 films from around the world.

Doku Fest

NGOM Fest is a music festival established in Prizren, Kosovo.The Festival originates from a group of young people who are active in the social life of Prizren and Kosovo.The first edition of the festival was held in June 2011 and due to its major success, the activists were more committed in organizing this event.

Ngom Fest

SUNNY HILL Festival is the biggest festival to be organized in Kosovo, set to become the biggest in the region. The Festival will be a continuation of two very successful concerts organized for two-times Brits Awards winner, DUA LIPA in the capital city of Kosovo, that gathered more the 40.000 people.

Sunny Hill Festival

Bunar Fest is a traditional festival, which is held in Prizren. Everyone can show themselves and participate in this festival and experience adrenaline in their blood.The festival publishes a comic book every year,the subject of the book includes various persona from the old urban legends of Prizren.
